## The Story

Panta Ilish — fermented rice served with fried hilsa fish — began as peasant food and has become a cultural symbol of Bengali New Year celebrations.

## Ingredients

- Leftover cooked rice, soaked overnight in water
- Fried Ilish (hilsa) fish
- Green chili, mustard oil, sliced onion

## Preparation

Soak the rice overnight so it ferments slightly. Serve cold, with the fish fried crisp on the side, alongside chili and onion.
