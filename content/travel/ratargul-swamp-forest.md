## Getting There

Ratargul is located about 26 km from Sylhet city, in the Gowain River basin. The forest is only fully submerged and navigable by boat during the monsoon season (June–September).

## What to Expect

Hire a small country boat from Motorghat. Local boatmen weave through half-submerged trees, giving the forest its nickname as the "Amazon of Bangladesh."

## Travel Tips

- Best visited early morning to avoid crowds and heat.
- Combine with a visit to Bisnakandi on the same day trip.
- Carry a dry bag — you *will* get splashed.
